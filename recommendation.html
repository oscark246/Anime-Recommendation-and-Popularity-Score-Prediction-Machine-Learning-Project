How Feature-Based Recommendation Works

The feature-based recommendation system allows you to select specific genres, types, and sources of anime to narrow down your preferences. You can also specify a minimum score to filter the results based on popularity and ratings. If nothing is selected, it will display the top 10 anime from the dataset.

Use this method if you have a general idea of the kind of anime you're looking for and want tailored recommendations based on your preferences.
